Introduction

The most secure and recommended way to get Media Contacts uploaded is to manually upload the contacts yourself. In some cases, you may prefer to force Media Contacts to register themselves. In this scenario, we allow you to use the Media Contact Registration Feature.

For those willing to register, they will display within your Media Contacts page with access to get invited to any promos.

In order to register, potential Media Contacts need to be aware of the registration page link. This could be obtained from an email invitation, a website, blog, social networking sites or any other means. We will provide the link below, but you must get the links to your prospective Media Contacts.

NOTE: The registration page is PUBLIC, so anybody, regardless of how they came across the page, could potentially register as a contact.

Because of the potential unwanted users to access your promos, you must confirm that you want to allow Media Contacts the ability to register.

This can be toggled on/off by checking/unchecking the checkbox

Generic Link

Provide this link if you DO NOT wish to automatically include new contacts into any Media Contact Groups.

https://margera.haulix.com/media/register
